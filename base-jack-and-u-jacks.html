Base Jacks and U Jacks

At DSS, both Base Jacks and U Jacks are available for hire basis in bulk quantity throughout the country, for construction purposes in every sector. Such scaffolding Jacks provide a method of support and adjustment which can be used at either ends of a scaffolding verticals.

Adjustable Base Jacks act as a base plate at the ends of a scaffolding network to support and secure the system on uneven surfaces.

The U Jacks are used as equipments for carrying steel or wooden channels which support the beams and slabs of a structure, the jack works along with an nut restraint to adjust the stem of the jack has minimum engagement of 8 inches inside the vertical scaffold member. Average Weight: 3kg
