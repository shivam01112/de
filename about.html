Delhi Scaffolding

Based in Delhi-NCR, Delhi Shuttering Stores, is one of the most prominent and reliable suppliers of Steel Shuttering and Scaffolding material across India. Holding experience of over 50 years, we have established customer bases in major cities like Delhi, Harayana, Mumbai, Calcutta, Punjab, Odhisa, Lucknow etc.

Our aim has always been to provide optimum material quality at competent rates, with swift and transparent communications and transactions, making Delhi Shuttering Stores, one of the most reputed names in supplying Steel Shuttering and Scaffolding material across India. Major construction companies prefer our products and nature of business because:

  • Huge savings in erection and dismantling times at minimal site cost.
  • Readily available material in all sizes and variants in bulk quanity
  • Fast and transparent transcation of material and communication
  • Significantly faster erection and minimum storage space on site.
