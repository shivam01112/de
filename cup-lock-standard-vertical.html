Cup Lock Standard | Vertical

Delhi Shuttering Stores, provides cuplock vertical members, commonly known as Standards using optimum quality MS Pipes (40MM D), these members are the fundamental load bearing members in cup lock scaffolding systems, and consist of top and bottom cup to lock the horizontal members in place.

The bottom cups are welded at a distance 0.5 meters on the vertical pipe, whereas the top cup can be moved and retained by a small welded fixture, consecutive vertical members can be attached using Spigot / Joint Pins, and can be fixed with Base or U jacks and either ends.
